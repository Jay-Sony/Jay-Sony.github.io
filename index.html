Jaden Weh-Palmer

Background: Newark Native and Programmer

Born in East Orange, New Jersey, Jaden would go on to be raised in Newark, New Jersey where he attended North Star Academy Charter School from kindergarden through high school. He'd begin expressing an interest in computer science eighth grade and would go on to take computer science related courses in high school. These courses included intro to computer science and mobile app development.

Projects and Extracurriculars: His Coding Journey

While attending high school, Jaden would spend his freshman and softmore year as a debator. It wasn't until junior and senior year that he began taking computer science courses. These courses included intro to computer science and mobile app development where he developed a MVP(Minimum Viable Product) of a collaborative social media platform centered around memes of all kinds for all people. In the summer of his junior going on senior year, he attended All Star Code, a summer intensive centered around educating men of color on coding and computer programming. This six week program allowed him to gain and builld upon his skills in javascript, html, and CSS.
